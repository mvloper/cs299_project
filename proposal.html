Project Proposal

We will be making a browser extension to add dark mode and high contrast mode to websites. Our goal is to make them easier to read/use without hurting your eyes.

Diagram:

Use Case Scenario:

With our browser extension installed, a user could select options to make a website darker by turning background elements dark gray and text/icons bright. Or they could make a website high contrast by turning background elements white and text/icons black and blue. These options would be accessed from the extensions menu in their browser. Our extension would provide multiple solutions to issues of websites causing eye strain or being difficult to read.
