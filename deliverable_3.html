High-Fidelity Prototype and Evaluation Plan

User Manual:

To operate, first click on the nightlight icon in the top right corner of the screen. This will launch a popup. From there, select from the following three modes: 1. Dark mode: This will change the background color of the site to a dark grey and the text to light grey, borders to a medium grey, and any containers (such as the background of the popup) to a medium grey. 2. High Contrast mode: This will change the background color of the site to a light grey, the text to a dark grey, borders to a medium-light grey, and any containers to a medium grey. 3. Custom Palette: From here, choose the colors you want to set by typing in the hexadecimal codes for the colors, leaving off the #. Then click submit, and the palette will be saved for your current session. When you are done, click on the icon again to close the popup.

Usability Goals:

New users should be capable of:

  • installing the extension to their browser
  • navigating and utilizing the extension within 15 minutes of use.

Benchmark Tasks:

  • access the extension from browser menu
  • Apply pre-built pallettes such as dark mode or high contrast mode to web page
  • Create a custom pallette in the extension and apply it to web page
